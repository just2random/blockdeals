FAQs
  • add_boxHow do I remove my deal?
    FOMOdeals uses blockchain technology and nothing can be deleted. Although you can hide the post from the FOMOdeals page simply by editing your post on steemit and add either the delete or delist tags to your post.
  • add_boxHow do I remove my comment?
    Click the [delete] button at the bottom of your comment on the details page just like you would any other steem comment.
  • add_boxHow do i edit my comment?
    Use the [edit] button at the bottom of your comment like you would with any other steem comment.
  • add_boxHow do I remove my vote?
    You can remove your vote within 48 hours of voting. Just click the vote button a second time to toggle your vote.
  • add_boxHow do I add my country to Blockdeals?
    We already support all countries, simply add a deal above and select your country from the list of countries. We only show countries that have deals available to reduce the size of our menus. Once you add a deal for your country it will magically start appearing in our menus! :)
